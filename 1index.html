1. Check your social media privacy settings

We all store a lot of personal data on social networks - and if you
use the defalit settings, literally everyone can see a significant
portion of this data. Therefore, check the privacy settings for all
social networks you use. What should be shown to everyone
and what can only be seen by your friends (or no one but you)
is up to you, not Mark Zuckerberg.

  • Change your social media privacy settings.
    Instagram Facebook, Vkontakte, Odnoklassniki,
    Twitter, LinkedIn and Instagram are all ways to do this.
  • From time to time, social networks change attitudes.
    So sometimes check if something new has appeared.
    For example, recently, Vkontakte has introduced a ban
    on the search under your profile by phone number -
    a useful option that we recommend.
